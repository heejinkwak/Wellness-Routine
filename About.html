About

Wellness Routine offers you the flexibility to excercise anywhere at your designated time.

There are no requirements or equipments needed. Simply use your device to visit this website and start excercising.

All you need is your personal space, and you are all set!

In just about 15 minutes of your time, you can enhance your daily well-being and be brighter than ever before.

Who?

- People who don't have time to go the gym

- People who are too shy to go to the gym

- Students who want to take breaks in between studying time

- People who want to learn simple excercise movements

- Anyone who wants to have a healthy body!

How?

- Simply click on the body part of your choice, and Wellness Routine will assist you with a quick and simple workout.

- Stretching is also included as it is a very important process during a workout.

When?

- In the morning

- During a lunch break

- Before going to bed

- Literally whenever you are free!
