The abs, a shortened term for abdominal muscles, is an important part of your body that is crucial for many functions. There are five main muscles in the ab, which are External obliques, Internal obliques, Pyramidalis, Rectus abdominis, and Transversus abdominis. These terms may sound too complicated. To summarize the functions of the ab muscles, it helps maintain posture, keeps the organs in place, and maintains consistent internal pressure. It also supports your spine and body while standing, sitting, twisting, and excercising. Basically having healthy ab muscles helps you keep your core balanced and safe.

Recommend to...

  • People who want increase stability, posture, and core strength.
  • People who want to reduce back pain.
  • People who want to lose belly fat!
