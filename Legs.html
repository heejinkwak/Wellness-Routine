The leg muscles are vital in supporting the overall body, maintaining posture, and facilitating movement. The range of leg muscles are wide, but it can be divided into two parts: upper and lower leg muscles. These muscles work together to ensure mobility, stability, and joint health, playing a crucial role in fundamental activities such as standing up straight and overall physical well-being.
